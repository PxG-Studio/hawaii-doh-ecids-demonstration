# Interview Demo Script — 10–15 Minute Presentation

## Phase 1: The Setup & Hook (Minutes 0–3)

**Your Pitch:**
> *"As an educator and coordinator, I know that tracking statewide compliance manually wastes hundreds of hours. Instead of just talking about workflow optimization, I brought a live, functional solution built as a modular extension inside Moodle to show how we can automate data tracking and meet federal regulations simultaneously."*

**The Hand-off:**
Hand the employer a tablet or show a QR code on your screen.

> *"If you scan this QR code with your phone, it will securely bridge you directly into our live simulated Edge Dashboard."*

## Phase 2: The Live Mobile Experience (Minutes 3–8)

As they look at the dashboard on their phone or tablet, guide them through:

1. **The Open Data Integration:** *"This dashboard is actively pulling from a mock open-source educational database via a custom REST API endpoint, mimicking real-world statewide metrics."*

2. **The Compliance Pivot:** Point to the real-time compliance tracking widget. *"It automatically highlights programs lagging behind on specific benchmarks, linking data directly to proactive intervention."*

3. **The Privacy Shield:** Point out the anonymization. *"While the backend tracks specific milestones, the UI uses strict data-anonymization scripts. This structure adheres to FERPA and utilizes NIST 800-53 security controls to ensure no Personally Identifiable Information is ever exposed over the network interface."*

## Phase 3: NotebookLM Audio Deep Dive (Minutes 8–12)

> *"To show how we can scale professional development and make technical guidelines digestible for staff across the state, I used generative AI to convert our dense compliance manual into an interactive, two-person podcast format. Let's listen to how a regional coordinator would experience our Moodle onboarding system."*

→ Play the 90-second NotebookLM audio segment

## Phase 4: Q&A and Close (Minutes 12–15)

Bring it back to core strengths:
- **Data analytics driving outcomes** — the compliance tracker doesn't just report, it predicts
- **Managing large-scale workflows** — automated pipelines replace manual tracking
- **Building institutional capacity** — teachers and coordinators use familiar tools (Moodle) extended with purpose-built modules
